3. What will I get?

If you are paid hardship, you will get less income-based Jobseeker's Allowance. The exact amount is based on your personal circumstances.

Does hardship have to be repaid?

People who get Jobseeker's Allowance do not normally have to repay hardship payments.

When will I be paid?

The date you will get a payment depends on your personal circumstances.

If you qualify, a hardship payment will be paid from either:

  • the same date you would have had a payment of Jobseeker's Allowance
  • 2 weeks after the date you would have had a payment of Jobseeker's Allowance

We will tell you when you will be paid.
