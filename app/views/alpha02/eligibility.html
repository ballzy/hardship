2. Eligibility

You may get a hardship payment if your income-based Jobseeker's Allowance has been stopped because:

  • you are not available for work
  • you are not actively seeking work
  • you do not have a Jobseeker's Agreement
  • there is a doubt about whether you meet any of the above conditions
  • your Jobseeker's Allowance has been sanctioned
  • a fraud penalty has been imposed

If you get contribution-based Jobseeker's Allowance you can still apply for a hardship payment. You can do this through your local Jobcentre.

If you are not sure what you are getting, contact your local Jobcentre.
