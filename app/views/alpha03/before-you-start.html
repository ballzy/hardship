To apply for Hardship Payment:

  • You must have had your income-based Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA) or income-related Employment Support Allowance (ESA) stopped because you have been sanctioned.
  • You must be in financial hardship. This means you can't afford to pay your bills, eat or to live anywhere.
